Our Plan to change South Sakwa Ward

The power to transform our ward is in your hands.

About

Hon. Micah Omondi is committed to creating a society that is fair, just, and sustainable. His mission is to empower communities, protect the environment, and ensure equal opportunities for all.

A Leader for the People – Hon. Micah Omondi is a true son of South Sakwa Ward. He understands the struggles and dreams of the South Sakwa Ward residents because he lives there. He listens, engages, and acts in the best interest of the people.

Our Goals

Agriculture & Food Security

South Sakwa is blessed with fertile land, yet our farmers struggle with low yields, poor markets, and expensive farm inputs. Hon. Micah Omondi will:
✅ Support farmers with subsidized fertilizers, seeds, and modern farming techniques.
✅ Promote agribusiness by linking farmers to better markets and value addition opportunities.
✅ Revive and strengthen cooperative societies to enhance collective bargaining power for our farmers.
✅ Ensure better livestock farming programs to improve dairy and poultry farming.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Our young people and women are the backbone of our community, yet they face limited economic opportunities . Hon. Micah Omondi will:
✅ Provide capital and training for youth and women-led businesses through ward-based empowerment funds.
✅ Promote technical skills training and job creation through partnerships with TVET institutions.
✅ Establish youth and women savings and credit groups (SACCOs) to enhance financial empowerment.
✅ Support local talents, sports, and creative arts to help young people build careers.

Education & Bursaries

Education is the foundation of a better future. Hon. Micah Omondi will:
✅ Increase bursary allocations for needy students to ensure no child is left behind.
✅ Support construction and renovation of classrooms to create a better learning environment.
✅ Ensure proper equipping of schools with books, desks, and essential learning materials.
✅ Promote ICT and digital literacy programs for students and job seekers.

Healthcare & Sanitation

A healthy community is a productive community. Hon. Micah Omondi will:
✅ Improve health centers and dispensaries by ensuring they have enough drugs and qualified staff.
✅ Push for free maternal healthcare for expectant mothers and better child health programs.
✅ Enhance clean water supply projects to reduce cases of waterborne diseases.
✅ Advocate for improved waste management to keep our environment clean.
