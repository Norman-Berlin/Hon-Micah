About

Hon. Micah Omondi is dedicated to creating a fair, just, and sustainable society. His mission is to empower communities, protect the environment, and ensure equal opportunities for all.

The power to transform our ward is in your hands. In 2027, let us vote for Hon. Micah Omondi , a leader with vision, action, and integrity. Together, we can make South Sakwa a place of opportunity, prosperity, and dignity for all!
